Safety First

Firecracker Safety Guidelines

Celebrate Deepavali safely, responsibly and as per local authority rules.

Customer Safety

Before blasting firecrackers

Simple colourful safety reminders for every family.

👨‍👩‍👧

Adult Supervision

Children should use fireworks only under responsible adult supervision.

🧯

Keep Water or Sand

Keep water, sand or a fire extinguisher nearby before lighting crackers.

🌳

Use Open Area

Use crackers only in open outdoor areas away from buildings and vehicles.

↔️

Keep Safe Distance

Move back immediately after lighting and maintain a safe distance.

🚫

Do Not Relight

Never relight a failed or half-burnt cracker. Wait and pour water safely.

👕

Wear Cotton Clothes

Avoid loose synthetic clothing. Prefer cotton clothes while bursting crackers.

🔥

Store Away from Heat

Keep unopened crackers away from flame, lamps, candles and heat.

🪔

Use Safety Lighter

Use a long incense stick or safety lighter. Avoid direct hand lighting.

🐾

Protect Animals

Do not burst crackers near pets, birds or street animals.

🏥

Avoid Sensitive Areas

Do not burst near hospitals, petrol pumps, crowded places or electrical rooms.

⏰

Follow Permitted Time

Follow local authority rules and permitted firecracker timings.

🧹

Safe Disposal

Allow used crackers to cool before disposal. Keep the area clean.
