Safety Guidelines

Celebrate Deepavali safely and responsibly with adult supervision and local authority compliance.

🧯

Keep water/sand nearby

Always keep a bucket of water or sand nearby while using fireworks.

👨‍👩‍👧

Adult supervision

Children should use fireworks only under responsible adult supervision.

🚫

Avoid unsafe places

Do not burst crackers near hospitals, vehicles, petrol pumps, animals or crowded areas.

📍

Follow local rules

Use crackers only during permitted timing and permitted locations as per local authority rules.

🧒

Protect children

Keep fireworks away from small children and store them in a dry safe place.

🔥

One at a time

Light only one cracker at a time and move away immediately after lighting.
